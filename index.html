Introduction To Software Engineering

Wima Institute is offering an intensive 8 weeks training program that will introduce students to the field of Software Engineering. With a focus on practical knowledge from the industry, the students will acquire the software development skills they need to excel in the modern workplace. The founder and lead instructor of Wima Institute has extensive experience in the IT sector. After graduating from Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT) with a degree in Computer Science, he worked for 10+ years as an engineer and product manager in multinational companies and Silicon Valley startups.

Fundamentals

This module will cover the fundamentals that underpin computer programming. The students will master the most used computer algorithms and data structures. They will also learn about the theory of computation and how to build secure and scalable applications.

Programming Languages

In this module, the students will solve problems and build projects using a variety of programming languages: C, Python, JavaScript, HTML, SQL, and CSS. The projects will be chosen from day-to-day scenarios that the students experience. The Django and Flask web frameworks will be introduced.

User Interface Design

In this module, the students will learn how to design and build user interfaces that are intuitive, secure, and responsive. In addition, they will leverage Git and the Continuous Integration / Continuous Deployment (CI/CD) processes to ship software with high quality standards.
