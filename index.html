Digital Skills Training

Wima Institute is offering a 6 weeks digital skills training program that will introduce young boys and girls to the field of Information Technology (IT). With a focus on practical knowledge from the industry, the students will acquire the digital skills they need to excel in the modern workplace. The founder and lead instructor of Wima Institute has extensive experience in the IT sector. After graduating from Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT) with a degree in Computer Science, he worked for 10+ years as an engineer and product manager in Microsoft and Silicon Valley startups.

Introduction to IT

This module will cover how to use computers and the internet, ensuring one’s security and safety while using the internet, how to create a career plan for the IT field and touch typing. We will also discuss the implications of the emerging Artificial Intelligence (AI) field.

Coding

This module will introduce students to the art and science of computer programming. We will cover the foundations of all programming languages including functions, events, loops, conditions, abstraction, variables etc.

Understand Technology

This module will enable the students to understand the basic components of technology. We will delve into the workings of hardware, software, the internet, multimedia, computer security, web development and computer programming.
